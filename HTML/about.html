About Us

The Name

The Bar4L Ranch was first purchased in 1969 by Clinton H. Long alongside his wife Avis "Jeannine" Long. They named the property after their four children: Larry, Lance, Lex, and Larisa.

Current Generation

Both Clinton and Jeannine have since passed, and the ranch is now in the hands of three of their four children -- the second youngest Lex had an untimely death in the summer of 2019.

The Purpose

The Bar4L Ranch serves as a retreat for the family. This self-sustaining sanctuary provides refuge for its namesake so long as they tend to the angus herd grazing the grassland.
