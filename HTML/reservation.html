HipCamp

Our reservation system is hosted by the 3rd-party vendor HipCamp to ensure visitors have a trusted and streamlined process when reserving their spot. We are opening up our private 300 acre cattle ranch so you can enjoy the eclipse with us and other eclipse enthusiasts with the following options:

  • RV and Campers - dry and dispersed camping, meaning we have designated areas for RV/Campers in one of our pastures. Consequently, there will be no hookups (no water, electricity or sewage hookups). We provide options for a 1-night stay on Sun, April 7th or a 2-night stay on Sat & Sun, April 6-7th
  • Tent or Cowboy Camping - you can choose between a 1-night stay on Sun, April 7th or a 2-night stay on Sat & Sun, April 6-7th
  • Day Pass - one day only on Mon April 8th, the day of the eclipse
